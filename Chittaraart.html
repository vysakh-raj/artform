Chittara Art

Chitra, also spelled as Citra, is an Indian genre of art that includes painting, sketch and any art form of delineation.
The earliest mention of the term Chitra in the context of painting or picture is found in some of the ancient Sanskrit texts of Hinduism and Pali texts of Buddhism
Many texts generally dated to the post-4th-century BCE period, use the term Chitra in the sense of painting, and Chitrakara as a painter
